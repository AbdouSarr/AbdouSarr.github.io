ABOUT ME

I'm a 19 year old software developer, designer, and marketer.

I was born in Ottawa, Canada, and have spent majority of my life there.

I've been coding since the 4th grade, and released my very first app at the age of 14.

My parents are from the West African nation of Senegal, and my sister is from the state of Texas, USA.

When I'm not working on projects I'm either playing sports, listening to music, hanging out with friends, or interacting with my fraternity.

  • Fun fact: I win every limbo contest I participate in...

EDUCATION

I am currently attending Carleton University until 2020 as part of the first cohort in a joint BCS Degree program with Shopify Inc. This program allows me to obtain my degree in Computer Science from Carleton, while obtaining real world experience from the vast codebases of Shopify. Previously, I attended Sir Robert Borden High School from which I graduated in 2016.

PROJECTS

Although I have been coding since a young age, I've only been releasing software since 2014. These projects include a variety of apps that have topped the worldwide charts and garnered the attention of celebrities like Snoop Dogg, and publications like Bloomberg.

  • iSRB (2014) - A high school schedule and news aggregator
  • Celestial (2015) - A varying frequency based relaxation app
  • eXplore (2017) - An application that crosses location sharing with augmented reality
